modern luxury.

  • At the heart of a bustling city, REXAR Hotel features a subdued getaway painted in unornamented, unembellished simplicity. Our choices in deep blues, greys and blacks make up the cornerstone of a modern yet comforting feel in a city like New York. In doing so, we have created a minimalistic break from the vibrancy of the outside. This is your home away from home.

a poolside escape.

  • Inspired by private Cabanas, we at REXAR hoped to create a poolside experience unlike any other metropolitan capital. We've sectioned off our pool area with the brilliant use of trees and fencing. It's vital we keep the privacy of our visitors under wraps (towel wraps) from the busy outside. The high volume of greenery in our poolside area works to balance the heavy concrete that fills New York to its brim. In doing so, it's comfortable, quiet, and the closest thing to a resort-like experience in the city.

classic continental twist.

  • Typical english muffins, yogurt and cup cereal have nothing on our premier selection of catered breakfast. We rotate between award-winning chefs, source only the highest-quality ingredients, and always keep produce fresh as part of your stay. Whether it's fresh truffle gouda from Paris or Florida's premier oranges, you can be sure to find only the best at REXAR.

About Us

REXAR Hotel is a prominent property management company fostering a fully-computerized reservation system to store and retrieve booking information details directly through our reservations system. REXAR provides a functional and automated hotel reservation process that makes it simple to book rooms for our customers and help our hotel keep better track of the reservations. We offer an easy, no-hassle booking process.
