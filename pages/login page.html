About Us

REXAR Hotel is a prominent property management company fostering a fully-computerized reservation system to store and retrieve booking information details directly through our reservations system. REXAR provides a functional and automated hotel reservation process that makes it simple to book rooms for our customers and help our hotel keep better track of the reservations. We offer an easy, no-hassle booking process.
